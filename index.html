About Me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Virginia, with a focus on transportation engineering. My work centers on using data-driven methods to understand and model transportation systems, including topics such as connected-vehicle data, traffic operations, and safety analysis.

I enjoy working at the intersection of transportation engineering and data science, and I am particularly interested in how large-scale trajectory and telematics datasets can inform better transportation planning and operations.
